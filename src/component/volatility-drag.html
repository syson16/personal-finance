Volatility Drag

Why 3x does not mean triple forever

Leveraged funds usually reset every day. They multiply each day's move, then compound from the new balance.

That means path matters. A choppy market can leave the plain index only slightly down while a 3x fund loses much more.

More context

A gain and an equal-sized loss do not cancel out. If an index goes from 100 to 110 and then falls 10%, it ends at 99. The 3x version goes from 100 to 130 and then falls 30%, ending at 91.

Leveraged funds can work in a strong, smooth trend. They are structurally fragile when the path is volatile, because losses compound from a smaller base and large daily swings create drag.
